PIPELINE

What it does

01

Neural prediction

Meta FAIR's TRIBE v2 predicts activation across 7 brain regions for every window of your video. Visual cortex peaks during the cuts. Language regions activate on the tagline.

02

Social simulation

1000 personas grounded in real-world entities react to your content — sharing, drifting, counter-narrating. Behavioral signal at scale.

03

Iterative optimization

Qwen3.5-27B reads both signals and writes the next variant. Loops until thresholds clear.

STACK

Built on AMD

  • Hardware1× AMD MI300X — 192 GB HBM3, AMD Developer Cloud
  • RuntimeROCm 7.2 · vLLM 0.17.1 (rocm/vllm container)
  • Variant tierQwen3.5-9B — variant generation + MiroFish agents
  • Orchestrator tierQwen3.5-27B — cross-system analysis + report generation
  • Neural encoderMeta FAIR TRIBE v2 — trimodal brain encoder, ROCm PyTorch

Zero NVIDIA in the inference path.
